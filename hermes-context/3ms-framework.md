# 3Ms Weekly Operating Framework

Borrowed from AIS-OS. Keeps the week honest.

## Weekly audit — Four Cs

Ask once a week: which of the Four Cs is weakest right now?

- **Context** — does the OS have current facts about what's happening?
- **Connections** — are the right people and systems reachable?
- **Capabilities** — is there a gap between what I need to do and what I can do?
- **Cadence** — is the weekly rhythm actually running, or has drift crept in?

Name one gap. Close it before the next audit.

## Weekly level-up rule

One automation surfaced, scoped, and shipped per week.
Not five half-built ones. Boring is beautiful. Workflows beat agents.

## Weekly financial pulse

Check UFCU statement: total owed, money in/out, spending by category, progress vs. summer debt plan.
Plutus runs this read. Osman approves any logged entries.
