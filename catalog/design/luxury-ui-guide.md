Luxury interfaces should feel calm, precise, and editorial without becoming a
static brochure.

Use restrained spacing, confident typography, clear product hierarchy, and real
decision surfaces. For watch or collectible concepts, show collection browsing,
trust signals, authentication cues, concierge actions, saved or compare states,
and a detail view.

Avoid generic dark gradients, vague hero copy, fake luxury claims, copied brand
layouts, and ornamental decoration that does not help the user inspect or act.
