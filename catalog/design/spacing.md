Use a consistent spacing scale across the app. Repeated cards, panels, toolbars,
and form groups should feel related.

Avoid cramped UI. Text, icons, and buttons need room to breathe. Also avoid huge
empty hero sections when the user needs the actual product surface.

Group related content through proximity. Separate unrelated content with clear
section spacing, dividers, or bands.

Interactive controls should not jump or resize when hovered, focused, loading,
or selected.
