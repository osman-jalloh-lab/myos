Use a restrained palette. Pick a small set of neutrals, one or two intentional
accent colors, and semantic colors for success, warning, and error.

Dark premium interfaces are a good default for Builder apps when the domain
supports it, but contrast and readability matter more than mood.

Avoid random gradients and one-note palettes. Gradients should support depth or
state, not fill space because nothing else was designed.

Use accent colors intentionally for primary actions, selected states, alerts,
and key metrics. Do not assign new colors randomly to every card.
