A user should know what will happen before clicking and what happened after
clicking.

Actions need feedback. Use changed labels, status messages, toasts, selected
states, disabled states, or inline results so the interface feels responsive.

Make destructive or high impact actions reversible where possible. In demo apps,
prefer archive, undo, reset, or confirmation states over permanent deletion.

Empty states should be useful. They should explain why the area is empty and
offer the next best action.

Navigation should be clear, predictable, and visible where the workflow needs
movement between views.
